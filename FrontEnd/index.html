What is CardioAI?

CardioAI is an integrated heart-health platform designed to monitor vital signs, analyze medical data, predict potential risks, and provide instant communication between patients and doctors. Using AI-driven models, CardioAI tracks heart performance trends and alerts both the patient and the physician when something requires attention.

Key Advantages of CardioAI

Early Complication Prediction

Our AI's predictive models identify potential cardiac complications long before traditional symptoms appear, enabling timely intervention.

Long-Term Data Analysis

Beyond immediate post-op, our system continuously analyzes long-term data trends, offering sustained oversight and adaptive care plans.

Personalized Models

Each patient receives a unique AI model built from their complete medical history, ensuring highly accurate and relevant insights.

Real-time Monitoring

Continuous, around-the-clock tracking of vital signs delivers instant alerts so care teams can respond the moment readings drift out of range.

Inside the CardioAI Platform

CardioAI is an advanced, AI-powered platform designed to revolutionize post-operative cardiac monitoring. By combining real-time vital tracking with predictive analytics, the system enables hospitals and clinicians to identify complications early, enhance patient safety, and improve treatment outcomes.

Our platform provides two fully integrated portals:

Patient Portal

Track vital signs, upload medical records, and receive AI-powered insights.

Doctor Portal

Real-time monitoring, risk predictions, and full medical history access.
